Andhra Pradesh: The Land of Temples and Beaches

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Andhra Pradesh is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and beautiful temples. From the serene beaches of Visakhapatnam to the spiritual vibes of Tirupati, the state offers a diverse range of experiences. The capital, Amaravati, is a modern city with historical significance, while Kadapa is known for its historical sites and natural beauty.
