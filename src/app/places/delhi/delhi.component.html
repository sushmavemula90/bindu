Delhi: The Capital City of India

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Delhi, the bustling capital of India, is a city where history and modernity coexist. From the majestic Red Fort to the towering Qutub Minar, Delhi boasts a rich heritage and vibrant culture. The iconic India Gate stands as a memorial to the soldiers who sacrificed their lives, while Humayun's Tomb is a testament to Mughal architecture and beauty.
